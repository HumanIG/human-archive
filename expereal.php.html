Expereal

Life visualized, moment by moment.

the idea.

Translate emotion into a distinct and inviting visual language.

the approach.

We gave users the ability to capture snapshots of their lives and tag the moment with an emotional rating, location, description, and photos. Users can then share these moments with others, or use the analytics engine to create a visualization of their emotions over the past weeks and months.

a simple swipe, and it's done.

A modern looking menu is hidden just off the screen. Recording an experience with a finger swipe gives you a water color inspired graphic that grows and changes color according to your feelings.

data visualization.
